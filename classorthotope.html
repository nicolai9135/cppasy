Detailed Description

Subclass of polytope, represents all polytopes with orthogonal edges only. Formally, an orthotope (or hyperrectangle or box) is the cartesian product of orthogonal intervals. Therefore it is possible to describe its surface solely through providing the corresponding intervals .
